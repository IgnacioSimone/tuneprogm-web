Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025  ·  ← Back to TuneProGM

1. Data we collect

When you request a trial or purchase a license, we collect your email address. We also generate an anonymous hardware fingerprint (a one-way hash of your machine's UUID) solely to enforce the one-machine-per-license policy. This fingerprint cannot be used to identify you.

2. How we use your data

  • Email: to deliver your license key, send important product updates, and (with your consent) occasional promotional offers.
  • Hardware fingerprint: stored locally on your machine and on our license server to prevent license sharing. Never sold or shared with third parties.

3. Third-party services

  • Dodo Payments — processes purchases. See their privacy policy.
  • Brevo — manages our email list. See their privacy policy.

4. Data retention

We retain your email and license data for as long as your license is active. You may request deletion at any time by emailing support@tuneprogm.com.

5. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at support@tuneprogm.com.

6. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated via email to active license holders.
